Tarot Card Meanings — Complete Guide to All 22 Major Arcana

Unlock the ancient wisdom of the tarot

The Major Arcana form the spiritual backbone of the tarot deck — 22 archetypal cards numbered from 0 (The Fool) to XXI (The World). Unlike the Minor Arcana, which reflect daily events, these powerful cards represent life's deepest lessons, karmic turning points, and the soul's journey toward wholeness. Whether you are a seasoned reader or just beginning your tarot reading guide, understanding each card's tarot card meanings — both upright and reversed — is essential for accurate interpretation. In this complete major arcana meanings reference you will find concise keywords, upright insights, and shadow (reversed) messages for every card.
