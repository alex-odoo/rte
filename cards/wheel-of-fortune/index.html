Wheel of Fortune — Tarot Card Meaning

Major Arcana X · Fire · Jupiter · Sagittarius

X — Wheel of Fortune Element: Fire Planet: Jupiter Zodiac: Sagittarius

Wheel of Fortune Upright Meaning

cyclesdestinyturning pointluckchangekarma

The Wheel of Fortune is one of the most powerful symbols in the tarot — a great wheel turning ceaselessly, carrying all things from rise to fall and back again. When this card appears, you are at a major turning point. Cycles are completing and new ones beginning. What has been dormant is about to awaken; what has been dominant is about to shift. The Wheel reminds you that nothing is permanent — not suffering, not success, not stagnation. It is the ultimate card of change, and its appearance signals that the universe is actively rearranging your circumstances. The deepest lesson of the Wheel is to find your still center while everything around you moves. Attachment to any fixed state — good or bad — is the source of suffering.

Wheel of Fortune Reversed Meaning

bad luckresistance to changestagnationbroken cycles

Reversed, the Wheel indicates resistance to change, a cycle that feels stuck, or a run of bad luck that tests your resilience. You may be trying to control what is fundamentally beyond your control. Alternatively, karmic patterns are repeating because the lesson has not yet been learned. The reversed Wheel asks you to look at what keeps cycling back — that recurring theme is your growth edge.

Wheel of Fortune in Love

In love, the Wheel upright signals a destined shift — meeting someone significant, a relationship entering a new phase, or a karmic connection revealing itself. It carries a sense of fate. Reversed, it may indicate a relationship stuck in a repetitive cycle (the same argument, the same pattern) or feeling that romantic luck has abandoned you.

Wheel of Fortune in Career & Finances

The Wheel of Fortune in career is an auspicious sign of positive change — promotion, opportunity, or a lucky break. Timing is on your side. Reversed, it suggests that career momentum has stalled or that external factors beyond your control are affecting your professional life.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the Wheel of Fortune tarot card mean?
The Wheel of Fortune represents cycles, destiny, and major turning points. It signals that change is coming — what has been stuck will move, and what has been rising may shift. It reminds you that nothing is permanent and to find peace within change.
Is the Wheel of Fortune a good card?
Generally yes — it signals positive change, luck, and destiny in motion. However, the Wheel is neutral by nature; it turns in all directions. Upright, it usually brings favorable shifts. Reversed, it can indicate stagnation or resistance to necessary change.
What does the Wheel of Fortune mean in a love reading?
In love, the Wheel signals a fated turning point — meeting someone destined for you, or a relationship evolving into something new. Reversed, it warns of repetitive patterns or cycles in love that need to be broken for growth to occur.
