The Chariot — Tarot Card Meaning

Major Arcana VII · Water · Moon · Cancer

VII — The Chariot Element: Water Planet: Moon Zodiac: Cancer

The Chariot Upright Meaning

willpowerdeterminationvictorycontrolambitionmomentum

The Chariot surges forward with unstoppable determination, pulled by two sphinxes (or horses) that represent opposing forces harnessed into a single direction. This card is pure willpower — the drive to overcome obstacles and achieve victory through discipline and focus. When The Chariot appears, you are being called to take decisive action, push through resistance, and trust that your determination is stronger than any barrier. This is not passive hope; it is active, muscular effort directed at a clear goal. The Chariot often appears during periods of competition, travel, or significant personal milestones. It promises success — but only if you maintain control over your competing impulses and keep your eyes on the destination.

The Chariot Reversed Meaning

lack of directionaggressionscattered energyobstaclesdefeat

Reversed, The Chariot indicates loss of direction, scattered energy, or aggressive pushing that alienates rather than achieves. You may be trying to force an outcome through sheer willpower when the situation calls for flexibility. Alternatively, obstacles feel overwhelming and momentum has stalled. The reversed Chariot asks: are you driving with purpose, or just driving fast?

The Chariot in Love

In love, The Chariot upright signals a relationship moving forward with determination — overcoming obstacles together, making definitive commitments, or pursuing someone with clear intent. Reversed, it may indicate one partner driving the relationship while the other feels dragged along, or aggressive pursuit that feels more like control than courtship.

The Chariot in Career & Finances

Career-wise, The Chariot is among the strongest success indicators — ambition rewarded, goals achieved, competition won. It favors high-energy, competitive environments. Reversed, it warns of burnout, workplace aggression, or pursuing professional goals so aggressively that personal well-being suffers.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does The Chariot tarot card mean?
The Chariot represents willpower, determination, and victory through discipline. It calls for decisive action and pushing through obstacles with focused energy. Success is promised if you maintain control and direction.
Is The Chariot a yes or no card?
The Chariot is a strong Yes card — it confirms that determination and action will bring the desired outcome. It is one of the most affirming cards for questions about achievement and forward movement.
What does The Chariot mean in a love reading?
In love, The Chariot signals a relationship moving forward with determination and purpose. Reversed, it warns of one-sided effort, aggressive pursuit, or trying to force a connection that needs more organic development.
