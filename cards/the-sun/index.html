The Sun — Tarot Card Meaning

Major Arcana XIX · Fire · Sun · Leo

XIX — The Sun Element: Fire Planet: Sun Zodiac: Leo

The Sun Upright Meaning

joysuccessvitalitypositivityconfidencecelebration

The Sun is the most unambiguously positive card in the entire tarot deck. A radiant sun shines down on a joyful child riding a white horse, surrounded by sunflowers. There are no hidden meanings, no shadows, no complications — just pure light, warmth, and celebration. When The Sun appears, it signals a period of success, vitality, confidence, and clear-eyed joy. Problems dissolve, truth shines through, and optimism is not just warranted but overdue. This card often appears after a difficult period (especially following The Moon) as confirmation that the darkness has passed. The Sun asks you to celebrate, to be playful, to express yourself without apology, and to share your light with everyone around you.

The Sun Reversed Meaning

temporary setbacksinner child blockedexcessive optimismdelays

Even reversed, The Sun remains positive — it is arguably the only card in the deck that cannot be truly negative. Reversed, it may indicate that joy is present but you are having trouble accessing it, that success is slightly delayed, or that excessive optimism is blinding you to minor issues that need attention. The light is still there; you just need to step out of your own shadow.

The Sun in Love

In love, The Sun is radiantly positive — happiness, playfulness, warmth, and a partnership that feels effortless and joyful. If single, it signals that a bright, warm-hearted person is entering your life. Reversed, the love is there but something small is dimming it — address it directly rather than ignoring it.

The Sun in Career & Finances

The Sun in career is one of the best cards possible — recognition, success, creative fulfillment, and professional joy. Your work is appreciated and your confidence is well-placed. Reversed, success is coming but may be slightly delayed; keep your spirits high.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does The Sun tarot card mean?
The Sun represents pure joy, success, vitality, and celebration. It is the most positive card in the tarot deck — no hidden meanings, no shadows. When The Sun appears, things are going well and optimism is fully warranted.
Is The Sun the best tarot card?
Many tarot practitioners consider The Sun the most positive card in the deck. Even reversed, it remains fundamentally optimistic. It signals success, happiness, and the dissolution of problems.
What does The Sun mean in a love reading?
In love, The Sun signals radiantly happy partnership — warmth, playfulness, and effortless joy. If single, a bright, positive person is entering your life. It is the best card you can draw for love questions.
