Temperance — Tarot Card Meaning

Major Arcana XIV · Fire · Jupiter · Sagittarius

XIV — Temperance Element: Fire Planet: Jupiter Zodiac: Sagittarius

Temperance Upright Meaning

balancemoderationpatiencepurposeharmonyhealing

Temperance stands with one foot in water and one on land, patiently pouring liquid between two cups in an endless, impossible flow. This card is the alchemist of the tarot — blending opposites into something greater than either alone. When Temperance appears, life asks for moderation, patience, and the middle path. Extremes — overwork or laziness, obsession or indifference, rigid control or total surrender — are all out of alignment. Temperance invites you to find the golden mean, the precise mixture of effort and ease that creates sustainable progress. This is also a card of healing, suggesting that integration of past experiences is underway and that harmony is being restored gradually.

Temperance Reversed Meaning

imbalanceexcesslack of patienceconflictmisalignment

Reversed, Temperance indicates imbalance, excess, or lost patience. You may be pushing too hard in one direction at the expense of everything else, or swinging between extremes without finding equilibrium. Relationships, work-life balance, and health are common areas affected. The reversed Temperance asks: where have I lost my center?

Temperance in Love

In love, Temperance upright signals a balanced, harmonious relationship built on mutual patience and compromise. Differences are blended rather than fought over. Reversed, it warns of imbalance — one partner giving too much, emotional volatility, or a lack of patience with each other's imperfections.

Temperance in Career & Finances

Temperance in career readings suggests steady, patient progress rather than dramatic leaps. Collaboration, diplomacy, and finding common ground with colleagues are favored. Reversed, it warns of workplace conflicts from impatience, or a professional life that has become all-consuming at the expense of personal well-being.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the Temperance tarot card mean?
Temperance represents balance, moderation, patience, and the art of blending opposites into harmony. It calls for the middle path — avoiding extremes and finding sustainable equilibrium in all areas of life.
Is Temperance a good card?
Yes, Temperance is a deeply positive card of healing, harmony, and patience. It signals that integration is underway and that taking a measured approach will yield the best results.
What does Temperance mean in a love reading?
In love, Temperance signals a balanced, patient partnership where differences are blended harmoniously. Reversed, it warns of emotional imbalance, one-sided effort, or lost patience with a partner.
