Strength — Tarot Card Meaning

Major Arcana VIII · Fire · Sun · Leo

VIII — Strength Element: Fire Planet: Sun Zodiac: Leo

Strength Upright Meaning

courageinner strengthpatiencecompassiongentle power

Strength depicts a figure gently closing the mouth of a lion — not through violence, but through calm, patient authority. This card teaches that true power comes not from domination but from mastery over one's own impulses. When Strength appears, you are being asked to face a challenge with courage, patience, and compassion rather than force or fear. The lion represents your raw emotions, desires, and instincts; the figure represents your higher self's ability to channel these energies constructively. Strength is not the absence of fear — it is feeling the fear and choosing love, patience, and persistence anyway. This card often appears when you need endurance for a long-term challenge rather than a quick victory.

Strength Reversed Meaning

self-doubtweaknessinsecurityraw emotionlack of courage

Reversed, Strength indicates self-doubt, loss of confidence, or being overwhelmed by emotions you cannot control. You may be giving in to anger, fear, or anxiety instead of meeting them with the calm inner authority this card represents. Alternatively, reversed Strength can show someone who has been patient for too long and needs to assert themselves more firmly.

Strength in Love

In love, Strength upright signals a relationship built on mutual patience, emotional resilience, and genuine compassion. Conflicts are resolved through understanding rather than argument. It also indicates strong physical attraction tempered by emotional maturity. Reversed, it may suggest insecurity undermining a relationship, or emotional exhaustion from always being the patient one.

Strength in Career & Finances

Strength in career readings indicates that quiet persistence will yield results — keep going even when progress feels slow. Your emotional intelligence is a professional asset. Reversed, it warns of losing confidence in your abilities or letting workplace stress overwhelm your composure.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the Strength tarot card mean?
Strength represents inner courage, patience, and gentle power. It teaches that true mastery comes from compassion and self-control rather than force. The lion symbolizes raw emotions that are tamed through love, not violence.
Is Strength a good card?
Yes, Strength is deeply positive — it confirms you have the inner resources to face any challenge through patience and courage. Even reversed, it is a call to reconnect with your innate resilience rather than a warning of failure.
What does Strength mean in a love reading?
In love, Strength signals a resilient, compassionate partnership where conflicts are resolved through understanding. It indicates strong attraction balanced by emotional maturity. Reversed, it warns of insecurity or emotional exhaustion in the relationship.
